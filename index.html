About me

I grew up in Georgia, and spent a year in Texas, but Utah is where I was born and have lived a majority of my adult life. When I'm not coding, you can find me on an adventure with my amazing wife and child.

Growing up I always had a fascination with technology. I was perplexed with how electronics worked using only natural elements and electricity. Desperate for answers, I began to take apart and examine various electronic components. Much to my parents dismay, not even the family vcr player was safe. Then one day I recieved my first computer and discovered the endless possibilities of software.

That love for technology persists today, and I have spent much of my life pursuing this passion. In college, I attended Utah Valley University where I received my Associates of Science. In pursuit of my bachelors degree I took many classes in life sciences, chemistry, and geology before discovering computer science. After several semesters in the CS program I felt like the curriculum was to slow and didn't teach enough modern web development concepts. As a result I decided to attend DevMountain's full stack web development program and I’ve never looked back!

Now I work for DevMountain as a full stack web developer and lead mentor. I specialize in Javascript frameworks & libraries, primarily working with React.js and Node.js. I am always open to new opportinities, so don’t hesitate to get in touch.
